SAHOLA Floral Art & Event Design is an upscale floral design studio founded by Olga Sahraoui, who brought new elements of European Design into New York style. SAHOLA – a derivative of her last and first name – driven by a passion for flowers and fearless quest for innovation. Olga’s creative flair and stylistic approach allows her to incorporate flowers into special events and weddings. Her impeccable taste and ability to manifest the visually stunning bouquets as well as the discretion with which she treats her clients has created a unique and lasting trust which ranks SAHOLA Floral Art & Event Design at the top of one of the best flower companies. Challenging new ideas, unusual materials and personalization is important to Olga. With the special inclination towards floral designs, she naturally developed an eye for details and artistic flower combinations. Olga doesn’t believe in ordinary, every day boring bouquets and centerpieces, she believes in unique floral couture. Olga has pursued her passion across the world, studying it to the highest international levels with the most influential Dutch designers.
